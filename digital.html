Digital Literacy

Basics of Digital Literacy
A digitally literate person can communicate, collaborate, and stay safe online using digital tools.

Digital literacy involves evaluating online information, practicing ethical behavior, and using technology effectively in daily life.

Lectures

1. Information Literacy

Ability to find and evaluate information online. Helps identify reliable sources and prevents the spread of misinformation.

2. Communication and Collaboration

Use of digital tools for effective communication and teamwork. Emphasizes respectful language and file-sharing platforms.

3. Digital Safety and Security

Protect personal information online through strong passwords, privacy settings, antivirus software, and awareness of scams.

4. Digital Ethics and Responsibility

Use technology responsibly by respecting others, giving credit to creators, avoiding cyberbullying, and practicing ethical online behavior.

5. Technology Use

Understand how to operate devices and software to improve productivity, communication, and access to information effectively.
