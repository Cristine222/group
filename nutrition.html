Health & Nutrition

Diet, Exercise, and Healthy Lifestyle Habits.
Good nutrition helps the body grow, stay strong, and fight diseases. Health also includes proper exercise, hygiene, and enough rest to maintain physical and mental wellness.

It focuses on eating a balanced diet that provides essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, proteins, carbohydrates, and fats.

Lectures

1. Balance Diet

A balanced diet means eating the right amount of different types of food. It includes fruits, vegetables, grains, protein, and dairy. Each food group provides important nutrients that the body needs. Eating a balanced diet helps maintain energy and overall health. It can also prevent diseases like obesity and diabetes. Drinking enough water is also part of a balanced die

2. Importance of Vitamins and Minerals

Vitamins and minerals are essential nutrients that keep the body functioning properly. They help support the immune system and keep bones strong. For example, vitamin C helps fight infections, while calcium strengthens bones. These nutrients are found in fruits, vegetables, and dairy products. A lack of vitamins can lead to health problems. Eating a variety of foods ensures you get enough nutrients.

3. Physical Activity

Physical activity is important for maintaining a healthy body. It includes exercises like walking, running, and playing sports. Regular activity helps improve strength, flexibility, and endurance. It also helps control weight and reduce stress. Being active can lower the risk of diseases like heart problems. People should aim to exercise regularly for better health.

4. Importance of Sleep

Sleep is essential for the body to rest and recover. It helps improve memory, focus, and mood. Lack of sleep can lead to tiredness and poor health. Children and teenagers need more sleep than adults. Good sleep habits include going to bed on time and avoiding screens before sleeping. Getting enough sleep supports overall well-being.

5.Hydration

Hydration means having enough water in the body. Water is important for digestion, circulation, and temperature control. Drinking enough water helps prevent dehydration. People can lose water through sweating and daily activities. It is recommended to drink several glasses of water each day. Staying hydrated keeps the body functioning properly.
