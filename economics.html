Economic Basics

Supply & Demand, Markets, and Personal Finance
Economics studies how people, businesses, and governments use limited resources to satisfy needs and wants.

It also explains how money, prices, and markets work in everyday life.

Lectures

1. Supply and Demand

Explains how prices are determined. High demand with low supply increases prices; high supply with low demand lowers prices.

2. Goods and Services

Goods are tangible items, services are activities provided by others. Understanding them helps explain how the economy functions.

3. Money and Banking

Money facilitates trade; banks manage savings, loans, and transactions, keeping the economy organized and stable.

4. Production and Consumption

Production creates goods/services using resources; consumption uses these goods to satisfy needs. Balance affects economic growth.

5. Market Types

Markets are places where buyers and sellers exchange goods/services. Types include perfect competition, monopoly, and oligopoly.
