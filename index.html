What is Education?

Definition

Education is the process of gaining knowledge, skills, values, and understanding through learning, study, and experience.

Importance

  • Builds knowledge
  • Creates opportunities
  • Develops thinking skills
  • Improves communication

Types

  • Formal – Schools
  • Informal – Daily life
  • Non-formal – Online learning

Benefits

  • Better life quality
  • Confidence
  • Goal achievement
